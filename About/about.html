DJ Henry Jacobs

Henry Jacobs is a Los Angeles based DJ known for Progressive house/Melodic techno and electrifying performances that blend rhythm and percussion. With 7 years of experience behind the decks, he has commanded dance floors at venues like Mainro, Liaison, Avalon, Sound, La' Mesa, Hyde, etc... Henry Jacob's sets are characterized by unique sound of Melodic Techno, consistently drawing crowds with special transitions and remixes.

A magnetic force in the Los Angeles electronic music scene, Henry Jacobs has established himself as a pioneering voice in Melodic techno/Progressive house since 2018. Drawing inspiration from Tale of us, Argy, Cassian, Fideles his signature sound fuses with dynamic control creating an immersive journey on every dance floor.

Henry Jacobs's journey began in 2018, developing a deep passion for electronic music. After mastering the fundamentals at Avalon, Liaison, he quickly gained recognition for his unique style and taste. This distinctive style has led to residencies at Liaison, Mainro, Hyde Bootsy Bellows..

Known for seamlessly blending Melodic Techno, Progressive house, Henry Jacobs's sets are a dynamic exploration of electronic music vibe. Whether commanding main stages or crafting intimate club experiences, each performance is characterized by chord progression and mixing.

Released original tracks/remixes on all music platforms and has spun over 100 restaurant and clubs all over Los Angeles!

Currently, Henry Jacobs is focused releasing a new album, while maintaining residencies at Mainro Hollywood, Hyde La.
